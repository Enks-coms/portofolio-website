Project Overview

BrightNest is a modern landing page created for a home cleaning service business. The goal was to build a professional website that builds trust and encourages customers to book cleaning service

The Problem

Many local cleaning business rely only on social media and lack a professional website that clearly explain their services, pricing, and booking process.

The Solution

I designed a clean and modern landing page with clear service sections, pricing information, testimonials, and strong call-to-action element to improve customer trust and conversation.
